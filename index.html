Our Philosophy

Rewyndr was founded on the belief that a picture is worth a thousand words, and when those words are connected to objects in a photo, a story is told, a problem is solved, a connection is made. All of our products leverage proprietary image-based technologies to create this enlightenment. For our consumer products, the value is measured in more meaningful personal communications, connections, and memories; for our business products the ROI is proven through better and more timely solutions to difficult problems.
